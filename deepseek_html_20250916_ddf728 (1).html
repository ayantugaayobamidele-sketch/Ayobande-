About RCCG Manual Family

Our Mission

The RCCG Manual Family portal provides students with secure access to study materials using unique access codes provided by administrators.

How It Works

  1. Administrators generate access codes with expiration dates
  2. Students receive their unique access code
  3. Students login with their credentials
  4. Access categorized study materials
