About RCCG Manual Family

Our Mission

The RCCG Manual Family portal provides students with secure access to study materials using unique access codes provided by administrators.

How It Works

  1. Register as a student with your details
  2. Login anytime to access your dashboard
  3. Use access codes provided by admin to unlock categories
  4. Access study materials in unlocked categories
