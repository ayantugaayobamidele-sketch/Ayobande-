About RCCG Manual Family

Our Mission

The RCCG Manual Family portal provides students with secure access to study materials using unique access codes provided by administrators.

How It Works

  1. Administrators create categories with access codes
  2. Students register with email and password
  3. Students enter access codes to unlock categories
  4. Access study materials through category buttons
